# Prototype Capabilities & Limitations

## Capabilities
- Role-based login: Customer/Provider/Admin
- Provider can create services and availability slots
- Customer can browse services, create bookings
- Stripe test payment confirms booking via webhook
- AI recommendation endpoint suggests service types/providers and stores AI logs

## Limitations
- Limited seed data (demo scale)
- Verification workflow may be simulated (document upload optional)
- No real payouts (Stripe Connect not implemented)
- AI does not provide medical advice; informational only
